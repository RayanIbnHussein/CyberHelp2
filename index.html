Welcome to CyberHelp

CyberHelp is a platform dedicated to providing support, resources, and awareness on cyberbullying. Our mission is to help victims, raise awareness, and prevent online harassment.

What is Cyberbullying?

Cyberbullying is the use of digital devices, social media platforms, and other online tools to intentionally hurt, harass, or intimidate someone. It can take many forms, including threats, spreading rumors, sharing private information, or excluding someone from an online group.

Our Services

  • 24/7 Support: We offer round-the-clock support to individuals facing cyberbullying through our chat system.
  • Educational Resources: Learn about different forms of cyberbullying and how to prevent it.
  • Reporting Guidance: Find out how to report online abuse and get help.
  • Mental Health Support: Connect with professionals to manage the emotional toll of cyberbullying.
